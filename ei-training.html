Skill four · for your reps

Steady the people around you.

When someone's heated — a teammate or a customer — climb the ladder in order.

01

Acknowledge

"I hear you." Let them know they've been heard before anything else.

02

Empathize

"That would frustrate me too." You're on their side, not the problem's.

03

Clarify

Ask questions. Find the real issue under the emotion before you swing.

04

Solve

Now — and only now — fix it. Solving before the first three rungs backfires.

Emotions are contagious. Don't take the bait, and don't match the heat — stay the calm one and the room follows you. That's the thermostat again.
